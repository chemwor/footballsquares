Everything You Need to Run Squares

Our platform takes the work out of managing boards so you can focus on the fun.

Unlimited Boards

Run as many squares games as you want. From weekly matchups to big championship events. You can organize multiple boards for different groups or occasions, all in one place.

Easy Sharing

Send out a single link and let players join your board in seconds. Share your board on social media or via email for instant access and engagement.

Player Picks + Host Approval

Players claim their own squares, you approve with one click and our system automatically highlights the winners when the game ends. The approval process is streamlined so you can manage requests quickly and efficiently.

Custom Rules

Add your own rules or notes once, and every participant sees them automatically. Tailor each board to your games needs and keep everyone informed with clear guidelines.

Email Notifications

Everyone stays updated with instant emails when squares are claimed, approved, or winners are announced. Notifications ensure no one misses an important update or result.

Organized Boards

No more messy paper grids. Everything is tracked neatly online, ready to revisit anytime. Your boards are always accessible and easy to manage, even after the event is over.
